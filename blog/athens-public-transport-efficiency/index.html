Athens Public Transport Efficiency

Sun Jan 14 14:15:30 UTC 2013

OpenTripPlanner have released OpenTripPlanner Analyst to analyse city transportation network GTFS data. The tool provides a visualisation of public transport travel times from a specific location. The following map shows how far you can travel from the centre of Athens by bus and metro in 30,60,90,120 minutes. Plan to include this as an overlay on zee.gr at some point.
